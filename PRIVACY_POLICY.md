# Privacy Policy for Constitution Vault

**Last updated: December 6, 2025**

## Introduction

Constitution Vault ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Voice Input**: When you use the voice chat feature, audio is temporarily processed for speech recognition. Audio data is not stored permanently.
- **Search Queries**: Text and voice queries submitted to our AI chatbot for legal information.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: We may collect device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting purposes.
- **Usage Data**: Basic analytics about feature usage (e.g., which laws you browse, chat interactions) to improve the app.

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide AI-powered legal explanations through Google Gemini API
- Convert speech to text for voice queries
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Troubleshoot technical issues
- Respond to legal information requests

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

### Google Gemini AI
- Chat queries are sent to Google Gemini API for generating legal explanations
- Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- No personal identification data is shared with Gemini

### No Sale of Data
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

## Data Storage and Security

- **No User Accounts**: The app does not require account creation or login
- **No Personal Data Storage**: We do not store personal information like names, emails, or phone numbers
- **Temporary Processing**: Voice recordings are processed in real-time and not permanently stored
- **Secure Transmission**: All API communications use HTTPS encryption

## Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

- **Internet Access**: To fetch legal content and communicate with AI services
- **Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO)**: For voice input features (only when you use voice chat)
- **Bluetooth**: For connecting to external audio devices (optional)

Permissions are only used for their stated purposes and you can deny them at any time.

## Children's Privacy

Constitution Vault is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app contains educational legal content suitable for all ages.

## Data Retention

- Voice inputs are processed in real-time and discarded after processing
- Chat queries are temporarily cached during your session
- No long-term storage of user queries or personal information

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Decline voice input permissions and use text-only features
- Clear app data from your device settings at any time
- Uninstall the app, which removes all locally stored data

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted within the app and on our GitHub repository. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Legal Disclaimer

Constitution Vault provides legal information for educational purposes only. The AI-generated responses are not legal advice. Consult a qualified legal professional for specific legal matters.
